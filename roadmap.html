Start Your Journey

0/6 Steps

University Shortlisting

Step 1 1 week

Research and select Italian universities based on your academic profile, preferred courses, and location preferences.

Checklist:

  • Identify 5-6 target universities
  • Check admission requirements
  • Verify English-taught programs
  • Review tuition fees & scholarships

Application Filing (Offer Letter)

Step 2 1.5-3 months

Prepare and submit your application documents to selected universities to receive offer letters. The documents you present decide your admission chances. Make sure you have distinctive CV and SOP.

Documents Needed:

  • Academic transcripts
  • English proficiency proof
  • ‚ö†Ô∏èStatement of Purpose
  • ‚ö†Ô∏èCV
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Passport copy
  • Passport size photo

Pre-enrollment

Step 3 1-3 month

Complete pre-enrollment procedures on the Universitaly platform to formalize your acceptance.

Key Actions:

  • Create Universitaly account
  • Submit pre-enrollment application
  • Upload required documents
  • Pay application fees (if any)

DOV / CIMEA

Step 4 4-8 weeks

Obtain Declaration of Value (DOV) or CIMEA certification for your academic qualifications.

Document Legalization:

  • Passport copy
  • Degree certificates
  • Academic transcripts
  • Apostille/legalization
  • Official translations

Regional Scholarship Application

Step 5 1-2 months

Apply for regional scholarships (DSU) and other financial aid opportunities available in Italy.

Scholarship Types:

  • Regional (DSU) Scholarships
  • Italian Government Scholarships
  • University-specific Scholarships
  • Erasmus+ Grants

Visa Application

Step 6 Normally: 45 business days

Apply for your Italian student visa (Type D) at the Italian Embassy/Consulate in your country.

Visa Documents:

  • Completed visa application form
  • Valid passport
  • University acceptance letter
  • Proof of financial means
  • Accommodation proof
  • Health insurance
